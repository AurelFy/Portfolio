Janus

— This was my first web project, I had to create a presentation website dedicated to a web personality.

01

01 01

It was structured around three pages — promotional, critical, and factual — with particular attention to visual identity, navigation coherence, and compliance with accessibility standards.

— After a lot of hesitation, I finally decided to work on MrBeast.

02

02 02

His strong online presence, distinctive visual identity, and the impact of his content made him an interesting subject to work on. His profile also offered a good balance between promotion and critical analysis, while allowing me to develop a recognizable graphic universe.

— The website's design follows the visual codes of MrBeast’s universe.

03

03 03

Vibrant colors, bold typography, and a dynamic layout inspired by his content. The critical page stands out with a shift in visual identity, using black and white to clearly signal its distinct purpose and emphasize the more reflective perspective it offers on the creator’s image.

— I started this project with just the basics in HTML, CSS and a bit of JavaScript.

04

04 04

I didn’t yet have the technical fluency needed to code a full site smoothly. So, I learned along the way by testing, troubleshooting and relying on online resources. This process helped me better understand site structure and gain confidence in my development skills.
